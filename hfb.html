Hugs for Brady is a non-profit organization that aims to cure Childhood Illnesses and contributes to Cancer Research.

One way Hugs for Brady raises money is by selling a product called, "The Brady Buggy Wagon". The wagon is a unique product, guiding our focus to make the site's navigation and donation for the Brady Buggy Wagon more direct and streamlined.

Research

We conducted tests on how users navigated through the predesignated parts of the site. The most common comments included navigation issues and doubt towards the organization’s trustworthiness.

The Problem

Potential donors and supporters find it challenging to navigate the current website, understand the full breadth of donation opportunities, and feel as if the interface is outdated. The site also lacks additional information that impacted the organization’s trustworthiness and motives.

Conclusion

Non profits organizations to have a welcoming and user friendly page. Enhancing visual elements and creating emotional resonance with the content effectively communicates the organization's mission and intent, while also building trust and rapport. A significant percentage of users relying on mobile devices continues to increase daily. Ensuring the redesign is mobile-friendly is essential for outreach, with accessibility letting users visit the site, anytime and anywhere. With the Brady Buggy Wagon donation process clearer, visitors can feel confident donating, contributing to the cause and their community at the same time.

Reflection

While our prototype has gotten more positive feedback from users, there are still some areas of improvement. For example, the donation form could be simplified so that users do not feel overwhelmed by all the text and text fields on the page, especially with how the process takes two pages to complete. We recognized that this could easily overwhelm users, despite how simple it is to fill out each section of the form. Also, adjusting the mobile version of the site so that users feel more comfortable typing their information out is another area where we can improve on.
